What is a Document Management System?

A document management system (DMS) is a software solution that enables organizations to store, manage, and track electronic documents and images.

Types of Documents

  • Text documents
  • Images
  • Videos
  • Audio files

Why is it Important?

A DMS helps organizations to reduce paper clutter, improve document security, and increase productivity.

Benefits of a Document Management System

  • Improved document security
  • Increased productivity
  • Reduced paper clutter
  • Easy document sharing and collaboration
